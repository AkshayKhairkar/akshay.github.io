Introduction

With the advent of internet , man can access the whole world with just a mere click of mouse ! True isn’t ? With the latest development in the field of web technologies & web technologies user experience has just touched a new level. Such advancements have paved a way for betterment of human life. Today the world wide web has become a platform for everyone to access any sort of information , convey their thoughts,etc. and bring the world much more closer !

About Blogless

Moving forward with the former thought , Blogless is a platform which provides out-of-the box articles based on several categories; may it be technology , politics , business , sports , philosophy , literature , astronomy or any thing else you may have never thought of. As the name itself suggests - Its a lot more than just a blog !!! Stay Tuned.

Some Tweaks

  • The search bar helps the reader to search for an article of his/her choice.

  • Articles are grouped in accordance to category & tags.

  • The RECENTS widget on the right-hand side displays the latest articles being published.

  • Readers can conveniently browse articles through the tag cloud

  • Based on the words per line the reading time for an article is automatically calculated.

  • Articles can be embedded with photos , links as well as videos from youtube also.

  • Liked an article ? Then what to wait for ? Sharing links are automatically enabled for every article.
