Now everyone's a Scientist !

03-01-2016 · 358 words · 2 minute read
Apps
Apps · Android · Google

Yes, indeed google cares. With its apps , services , technologies & bla bla bla benefiting every hook and corner of the world. The tech giant has brought something interesting to its family of Android apps. Science Journal , as the name suggest is a needy app for all the geek scientists , makers , students , teachers and everyone around.

About the App

Science Journal is an Android based application developed by Google to help people gather data from the world around them. Well it might seem a quite un-understandable but to make it simple, the app is analogus to the Science Journals being used in laboratories to conduct experiments. As the journal keeps a track of all the observations being performed over and out so is this app. The only difference being that , its a quite smarter.

The app makes use of the in-build phone sensors such as accelerometer , ambient sensor , thermo sensor,etc. to provide real time statistics. Users can record such readings as per the need of their experiments. Readings are provided in graphical as well as numerical format , thus making it convenient for the users to understand them and organize their questions and ideas by getting rid of unnecessary paper records and expensive hardwares.

In case of some inspiration you need to get started with , Google has as entire guide of activities you could try with your free app.

Features

  • Explore your world Use the sensors in your Android phone to measure sound , light and more.

  • Organise projects Set up projects and experiments to organise your data and compare your results.

  • Record in real time See something you want to measure ? Record your observations in real time at the press of a button.

  • Add observational notes While you are recording, you can add notes and pictures to give more context to the data.

Quick Tweaks

  • License : Freeware
  • Latest Version : 1.0.89
  • App Size : ~ 12 MB
  • App Link : Science Journal

So what to wait for ??? To get started , all you need is an Android phone with the science journal app installed !
